Is Rehobeth Beach up to snuff?

Rehobeth Beach which was supposedly a best keep secret for beaches ain't no secret anymore. The beach is packed and bustling with people. There are great beach views to soak in and an endless boardwalk too. If the beach isn't for you, there is the amazing tax free shopping to be done in Rehobeth Beach thanks to Deleware! Three different shopping outlet and supposedly some of the largest liquor stores (disproven now) grace Rehobeth Beach.

Endless activites where there wasn't much time for lounging. Beach, shopping, early morning fishing trips, arcade, bowling, and endless eating with 4 meals a day. Activites upon activites. This was a trip that flew by becausing of the all the things to be done.
