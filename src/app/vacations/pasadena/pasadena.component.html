So what does the fishing and views actually look like in Pasadena

Pasadena is located at in northern Anne Arundel County; bordered to the north by the city of Baltimore, to the east by the tidal Patapsco River and by Riviera Beach, to the southeast by Lake Shore, to the south by Severna Park, and to the west by Glen Burnie. This secluded place is quiet, relaxing and has a great view of a river flowing into the Chesapeake Bay. Fishing was great with a private dock with catfish, perches, and other smaller fish were taking the bait. Night fishing was also quite relaxing with the soothing sounds of the ocean/river waves. Beautiful views of the the sunset even though the home is facing east. Food was so-so in this area but company and a great home more than makes up for it.

For this time around, special guests Grandpa and Grandma Ong graced us with their presence. Grandpa Ong was particulalry excited about gambling at the MGM Casino at National Harbor. His last trip netted him with a profit of roughly $200. Again, Grandpa Ong strikes again and makes another $200 after a rough run. Starting with $300, winning at the 2nd round of roulette with a $75 profit then losing it in subsequent rounds to down to $195. Little Knife then decided to play his hand at roulette but losing roughly $60. After a semi all-in bet win on red, we recouped our losses for a total of $175. God of Gamblers showed up and came out to win 2 crazy hands (one with a revenue of roughly $250) to claw back from $175 to a total of $500. Grandma Ong was chilling this entire time doing whatever she wanted.
