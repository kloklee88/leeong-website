So what's going on in Deep Creek

Deep Creek Lake is a four season paradise, offering fun on the lake in the summer sun, stunning autumn colors during fall, fluffy snow for downhill skiing at Wisp Resort, and fresh mountain air in the spring. There's always a reason to escape to the lake!

Being next to a nice freshwater lake and driving around in a boat is a a blast. it's a quiet spot where you can relax and lounge and not worry about work. Although at first there might not be a ton to do at first glance, when you really get to visit you realize that you have the most exciting and fun place on earth to go to... WALMART. A trip here, a trip there, a trip everyday, yay! Walmart has got your back.
